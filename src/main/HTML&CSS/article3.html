The Battlefield of Food Waste



Sidst opdateret d. 23/05/2035
Skrevet af: Herman Li

We step onto the battlefield,
Against a foe we've known for long,
It's a war for food we cannot yield,
We cannot tolerate waste and wrong.

A World at War

We see a world at war,
Against a hostile waste so deep,
Millions of tons of food we ignore,
It can't continue, it's time to take the leap.

Fighting for Resources

A fight for water, land, and power,
That's been lost due to our ways,
We can't accept this final hour,
We must stand up, take charge and seize the day.

The War on Waste

We can start with tiny steps,
Saving food, planning and responsibility,
Let's battle waste and win the bet,
For our planet's future and sustainability.

We cannot wait any longer,
We must act now before it's too late,
Let's fight for a brighter future, stronger,
And win the battle against waste and its hate.

For on the battlefield of food waste we stand,
Against a foe we can overcome,
With responsibility and action at hand,
We can win the war and a brighter future will come.
